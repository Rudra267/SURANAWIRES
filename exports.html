Key Export Products

1. Galvanized and Welded Wire Mesh

Applications: Our welded wire mesh products are used in a wide range of applications, including concrete reinforcement, construction fencing, and general industrial uses. They provide high strength and durability, even in harsh environments.

Features: Corrosion-resistant, robust, and versatile, our wire mesh products meet international standards and offer superior performance for both residential and commercial applications.

2. Barbed Wire Solutions

Applications: Barbed wire is widely used for security and perimeter fencing in sensitive areas such as borders, military zones, factories, and private properties. It offers exceptional resistance to wear and tear.

Features: Our heavy-duty galvanized barbed wire ensures longevity and optimal protection against intrusions, making it ideal for securing perimeters in both urban and remote areas.

3. Custom Wire Products

Applications: Surana Wires specializes in producing tailor-made wire solutions to meet the unique requirements of various industries, including construction, agriculture, telecommunications, and more.

Features: We offer custom wire products in a variety of sizes, strengths, and coatings to suit specific needs. Whether for reinforcing concrete, securing properties, or protecting crops, our custom solutions are designed to deliver the highest quality performance.

4. Agricultural Wire Mesh

Applications: Designed for crop protection, livestock enclosures, and fencing, our agricultural wire mesh products offer durability and weather resistance, ensuring long-lasting protection in farming environments.

Features: Flexible, corrosion-resistant, and strong, our agricultural mesh is ideal for sustaining various agricultural practices and environments.

5. Wire Mesh Gratings

Applications: Used in heavy-duty industrial and manufacturing settings for flooring, grating, and safety applications, wire mesh gratings are vital for creating safe, efficient working environments.

Features: Our high-strength wire mesh gratings offer durability and safety in industrial applications, particularly in areas with heavy foot traffic or equipment.
