About Surana Wires Private Limited

A Legacy of Excellence Since 1974

Founded in 1974, Surana Wires Private Limited has over 40 years of expertise as a leading manufacturer of high-quality wire and fencing products in India. With state-of-the-art facilities in Nacharam, Rangareddy District, and Chegunta, Medak District, we produce over 26,000 tons of galvanized wire and welded wire mesh annually, delivering robust and reliable solutions across industries and reinforcing our reputation as a trusted name in wire manufacturing.
